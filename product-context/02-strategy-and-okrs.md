# Strategy & OKRs — FleetMate

## Strategic role of FleetMate

FleetMate plays three strategic roles:

1. **Operational backbone for SMB carriers**
   - Replaces spreadsheets and WhatsApp as the coordination layer
   - Becomes the daily operational hub for owners, dispatchers, and drivers

2. **Driver adoption engine**
   - Free driver app drives organic installs at scale
   - Each driver install is a lead-gen surface for fleet owner conversion

3. **Data and monetization platform**
   - Route, cost, and compliance data = premium analytics products
   - Subscription tiers unlock automation, integrations, and reporting

---

## Strategic themes (current)

- **Reliability first** — "just works" is table stakes; instability kills trust in SMB
- **Dispatcher–driver connectivity** — real-time coordination is the core differentiator
- **Cost visibility** — fuel, tolls, delays: give owners the numbers they don't have today
- **Offline resilience** — drivers operate in low-connectivity environments; offline must be seamless
- **Conversion from free to paid** — grow the paying fleet owner base from the driver install base

---

## OKR alignment (illustrative — current quarter)

### Objective: Make FleetMate the most reliable SMB fleet tool in EU

- KR: Crash-free rate ≥ 99.2% across driver app (iOS + Android)
- KR: Navigation accuracy complaint rate < 0.5% of active sessions
- KR: App Store rating ≥ 4.5 in top 3 markets

---

### Objective: Grow active fleet subscriptions

- KR: Increase Monthly Active Fleets (MAF) by 20% QoQ
- KR: Improve trial-to-paid conversion rate from 18% to 28%
- KR: Reduce churn of paying fleets to < 3% monthly

---

### Objective: Deepen dispatcher–driver connectivity

- KR: % of trips executed with full route sync (dispatcher → driver) ≥ 65%
- KR: In-app message response rate (driver side) > 70% within 15 min
- KR: Dispatcher task assignment → driver acknowledgment time < 5 min (p75)

---

## Non-goals (explicit)

- Enterprise TMS integrations (SAP TM, Oracle) — not our segment
- OEM or embedded navigation deals
- Insurance or financing products (evaluated but out of scope this year)
