# Pricing & Monetization — FleetMate

## Business model

FleetMate operates a **freemium B2B2C model**:

- **Driver app**: Free. Core navigation and trip execution. No paywall.
  - Strategy: maximize driver installs → network effect → fleet owner discovery
- **Fleet subscription**: Paid. Dispatcher + owner web platform + advanced features.
  - Strategy: convert fleet owners from free trial; upsell on vehicle count and features

---

## Subscription tiers (current)

| Tier | Price | Vehicles | Key features |
|------|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Free** | €0 | — | Driver app only. Navigation, assigned trips. |
| **Starter** | €49/mo | Up to 5 | Live tracking, basic dispatch, cost log |
| **Growth** | €149/mo | Up to 25 | Full dispatch, offline sync, cost analytics, API |
| **Scale** | €349/mo | Up to 100 | All features + priority support + custom reporting |

*All paid tiers include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start trial.*

---

## Monetization logic

**Primary revenue driver**: Fleet subscriptions (MRR)

**Conversion funnel**:
1. Driver installs free app (organic, word-of-mouth)
2. Fleet owner discovers FleetMate via driver → signs up for trial
3. Trial → Starter conversion within 14 days (target: 28%)
4. Starter → Growth upsell when fleet exceeds 5 vehicles or needs analytics

**Secondary revenue** (future):
- Fuel card partnerships (referral + data)
- Toll integration marketplace
- Compliance reporting add-ons

---

## Pricing principles

- Price per fleet (not per seat) — reduces friction for SMB buyers
- Freemium driver app is non-negotiable — it is the acquisition channel
- Trial length is 14 days — long enough to see value, short enough to force a decision
- No long-term contracts at Starter; annual option at Growth/Scale (15% discount)
