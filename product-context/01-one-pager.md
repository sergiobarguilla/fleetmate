# FleetMate — Product One-Pager

## What the product is

FleetMate is a **fleet management SaaS platform** for small and mid-size road transport companies.
It gives fleet owners, dispatchers, and drivers a single, connected workspace to plan routes,
track vehicles, manage costs, and coordinate in real time.

FleetMate replaces fragmented tool stacks (spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, standalone GPS trackers)
with one purpose-built system designed for companies operating 5 to 100 trucks.

---

## Who it is for

**Primary users:**
- Fleet owners / managers — run the business, need visibility and cost control
- Dispatchers — plan and coordinate daily operations
- Drivers — execute trips, need clear instructions and reliable navigation

**Target company profile:**
- SMB road transport companies
- 5–100 vehicles
- Europe-focused (Poland, Germany, Czech Republic, Spain primary markets)
- Mix of employed drivers and owner-operators

---

## Core user job-to-be-done

> "Run a lean, reliable transport operation — know where every truck is, what every trip costs, and that every driver has what they need to do their job."

**Fleet owner job:** Control costs, utilisation, and compliance without spending the whole day on the phone.

**Dispatcher job:** Plan and assign trips efficiently, react fast to changes, keep drivers unblocked.

**Driver job:** Know exactly where to go, what to do, and get home on time — with minimum friction.

---

## Current product positioning

FleetMate today is:
- **Freemium mobile app** for drivers (navigation + task execution)
- **Subscription platform** for fleet owners and dispatchers (web + mobile)
- Positioned against fragmented tool stacks, not enterprise TMS systems

Strategic direction:
> From "GPS tracker + navigation" → "Integrated fleet operating system for SMB carriers"

---

## Why this product matters strategically

- SMB transport is massively underserved by complex, expensive enterprise software
- Driver and dispatcher coordination is the #1 daily pain point in this segment
- Strong network effect: one fleet owner subscription = multiple driver installs
- Data flywheel: route, cost, and compliance data improves with scale
- Large addressable market across EU road freight (est. 600k+ SMB carriers)
